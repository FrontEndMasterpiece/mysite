In this episode Z visits quarantine country. Z and the Band did not know where to find golden saxophone reeds. Minister Love discovers he can no longer cover costs for church bills. He is faced with the decision to close the church after 20 years of service or be evicted. The Minister bumps into Z and the Band at a neighborhood barbershop and happens to know about the golden saxophone reeds. The caller promotes a donation idea to Z and the Band. Alongside Minister Love, he plays his magical golden reeds across the land. While Z and the Band are still playing, the city is throwing money into the collection box.
